QuizVerse: Enhancing User Engagement Through UX Redesign

Role: UX Designer Tools: Figma, Miro, Adobe XD Duration: 3 weeks

The Problem

Users found existing quiz applications monotonous and disengaging, leading to high drop-off rates and low retention. The challenge was to create an immersive experience that would keep users coming back.

Research & Insights

Through user interviews and competitor analysis, I identified key pain points: lack of personalization, poor feedback mechanisms, and uninspiring visual design.

Solution

Designed a personalized quiz experience with adaptive difficulty, engaging animations, and social sharing features. The redesign resulted in a 40% increase in user engagement and 25% higher completion rates.

Travel Guide App: Simplifying Discovery Through Map-Based Design

Role: UI/UX Designer Tools: Figma, Adobe Illustrator Duration: 4 weeks

The Challenge

Travel apps often overwhelm users with information, making it difficult to discover relevant points of interest and plan itineraries efficiently.

Research Approach

Conducted user interviews and created personas to understand different travel styles. Analyzed competitor apps to identify gaps in the market.

Design Solution

Created an intuitive map-based interface with layered information, personalized recommendations, and offline functionality. The clean UI prioritized accessibility and ease of use for travelers on the go.
